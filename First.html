About Us

We Provide The Best
AirBnB For You !

We are your trusted brand in short term let's. Located at Tantra Hills,we offer luxury with value, with high class furnishings,swimming pool and rooftop bar. Home away from home. 30 minutes away from the airport and Osu. 10 minutes from Achimota mall which is one of the biggest shopping mall in the country providing you with absolute convenience and easy access to most of the exciting places in Accra. We have an in house driver who can take you to most places for a small fee.

The Space

We have an ensuite. It comes with two living area. One down Stairs and another upstairs which are shared by guest so there's some sort of privacy. There's is a kitchen which is shared with other guest. We have an inhouse cook who can prepare you lunch and dinner for a small fee

Other things to note

The property is a walking distance to a local shops.we also offer a car and a chauffeur upon request for a small fee. Bolt and uber operate in the area so it's easy to order for one.

Guest contributes 120 Ghana cedis a week towards electricity. If guests are couples they pay 150 Ghana cedis towards electricity
